Textbook (Wikipedia inspired) Piña Colada

18 January, 2021 Skill: 0-easy

Ingredients

  • 1 part white rum
  • 1 part (Alpro) coconut drink
  • 3 parts pineapple juice
  • Ice

Method

  1. Add ice to shaker
  2. Pour in all liquids
  3. Shake for an arbitrary amount of time
  4. Profit!
